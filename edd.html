About Us

Entrepreneurship has become crucial at the international level. Even under the changing economic scenarios it is perhaps the most crucial step in the development and progress of our nation. The Entrepreneurship Development Drive hoped to ensure establishment and upscaling of entrepreneurial bodies in your college, for an overall development and propagation of entrepreneurial culture. We want to establish a pan-India network of E-Cell's for facilitating sharing of ideas, thoughts and a boost for budding entrepreneurs.

Why EDD?

Identification and Utilization of Huge Pool of Resources

Your reach in new market increases and this empowers the association you have built up with your stakeholders and clients.

Unifying the Institutional Startup Ecosystem

Setting up the start-up ecosystem in an organized fashion through knowledge sharing and common help will help accelerate the attainment of growth. There can be a proper setup to support curate new ideas/startups/ventures.

Developing Entrepreneurial Roots

This drive provides different colleges in an interdisciplinary collaborative environment through which they can organize themselves to improve their understanding of the entrepreneurial ecosystem.

Enhancement

As the saying goes, two heads are better than one. Ideas can come from anywhere and sharing of innovative ideas to help improve

Extensive Network Growth

With us having a proper channelized and unique network in our respective regions, and with each of them continuing to build a strong network

FREE PASSES TO E-SUMMIT

Free passes to the paid events and workshops of E-Summit which have their own worth

CERTIFICATES

Last but not least a Certificate from E-Cell, IIT Madras.

Frequently Asked Questions

How to register ?

Fill the form after clicking the button below. Our team will contact you on your given mails with second round for the final selections. You can take part if you already have an established E-Cell or want to set up one in your college.

Can I enroll ?

It is for college students who aspire to create and upscale an entrepreneurial body in their college which will help other students of their college when they want to experience the adventurous life of an entrepreneur.

What process will be followed in this program for development?

We will focus on practical experience rather than providing just materials and lectures(there would lectures definitely as they are essential for growth :P ). We will also focus on ground work where you will be supposed to actually do what is taught as practical implementation and lecture knowledge are two different things.

How much time do we need to complete this project?

It is an year long project and time spent purely depends on your dedication to do the tasks allotted and your own efficiency.
